## Tips for Travelers

1. **Check your entry port** — Make sure you're arriving through one of the designated ports listed above.
2. **Have your onward ticket ready** — For 240-hour transit, you'll need proof of onward travel to a third country.
3. **Know your region** — Some ports allow travel within the entire province, while others may have specific zones.
4. **IATA codes matter** — When booking flights, use the IATA codes above to ensure you're flying into an eligible airport.
