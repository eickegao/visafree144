Hey, world travelers! China just made your layovers and quick visits way more exciting. The country has upgraded its visa-free transit policy—instead of the old 72-hour or 144-hour limit, you now get a whopping 240 hours (10 days)! 🎉

## What's New? Let's Break It Down!

| 🌏 Feature | ✨ Before | 🚀 Now |
|------------|----------|--------|
| ⏳ Visa-Free Stay | 72 or 144 hours | 240 hours (10 days!) |
| ✈ Eligible Countries | 54 countries | Still 54, but now with more perks! |
| 🛬 Entry Points | 39 designated airports | 60 airports! (Hello, more travel options!) |
| 🏙 Where You Can Go | 19 provinces/regions | 24 provinces/regions—more adventure unlocked! |

## New Airports on the List! 🛫

Now, you can land visa-free at 21 additional airports, including:

✅ Taiyuan (because why not Shanxi?)
✅ Wenzhou & Yiwu (shop 'til you drop!)
✅ Fuzhou, Quanzhou & Wuyishan (tea lovers, rejoice!)
✅ Jinan, Yantai & Weihai (coastal charm unlocked!)
…and many more!

## Where Can You Roam? 🗺

China has now expanded the areas you can explore, adding Shanxi, Anhui, Jiangxi, Hainan, and Guizhou to the visa-free travel map. Plus, in some provinces, you can now travel freely across the entire province instead of just a few cities.

## Why Should You Care?

This means less paperwork, fewer visa headaches, and more dumplings, pandas, and Great Wall selfies! 🥟🐼🏯

So, if you've been dreaming of a quick China trip without the visa hassle, now's your chance. Pack your bags and book that flight—China is waiting! 🇨🇳✨
